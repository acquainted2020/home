China Gordan Technology Alliance (CGTA) was founded on July 1, 2019, announced its dissolution on June 15, 2019 due to special reasons, and was reorganized on September 1, 2021. CGTA is composed of a group of network information security enthusiasts. Our responsibility is to help major network vendors find network security vulnerabilities and provide corresponding solutions. At the same time, when the country's network information security is violated, we must do our best to protect it. Our aim is to provide a technical discussion community and learning platform for "white hats" who love computers. Everyone is welcome to join the alliance to communicate.
